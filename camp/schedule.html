The Schedule

First things first. When booking your travel, please be careful to allow sufficient driving time to our somewhat remote destination. Hop over to our FAQ for more info.

Here's a quick look at what we have in store for you at Offline Camp. We'll share more detail as we get closer to the event.

Friday, April 28 – Arrive and get comfy

Campers may arrive at the ranch between 5:00 pm and 6:30 pm to start getting settled and exploring the property. Dinner will start promptly at 7:00 pm, followed by a welcome from your organizers.

Saturday, April 29 – Unconference sessions, passion talks, and more

Topics for unconference sessions will be proposed and voted on by campers on site, and participants have the opportunity to present 5-minute passion talks on any topic they're excited about. Bring your ideas for both!

Sunday, April 30 – Unconference sessions, passion talks, and more

Unconference sessions and passion talks continue, with time mixed in as always for conversation and relaxation. Sessions will end before dinner on Sunday, should anyone need to leave us early.

Monday, May 1 – Head home

Head home around 11:00 am, after breakfast and a final group standup.
